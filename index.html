Who I am

About Me

Hey — I'm Oguzhan, a junior Computer Science student at Middle Tennessee State University with a passion for building things that actually work at scale. I specialise in backend systems with Python and Django, but I'm equally comfortable wiring up a React frontend or diagnosing why a PostgreSQL query is crawling.

Before coming to the US, I did a year-long internship at Ynamdar, one of Turkmenistan's leading e-commerce companies, where I helped build Ynamdar Cars — a full car-selling platform — using Flask and PostgreSQL. That early production experience taught me more about software than any textbook ever could.

Outside of code I'm a multilinguist — I speak English, German, Russian, Turkish, and Turkmen — which gives me a weird superpower when working with international teams and documentation.
